About us

Ideasandvisions Solutions was established in 2020 as a sole proprietor firm, fully licensed by
the Institute of Certified Public Accountants (ICPAK) as of March 2021.

Our Mission Statement:
We provide sophisticated business solutions to our clients by imparting on our client’s years
of professional audit expertise and data analysis done timely with objective integrity.

Our Vision Statement:
To be recognized as the regional accounting firm of choice that provides comprehensive
financial and professional services to small and medium-sized businesses as well as individuals
while ensuring that their documentation of success, growth and expansion occurs in full and
demonstrable compliance with applicable law.
